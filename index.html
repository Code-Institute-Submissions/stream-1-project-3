Meet the Band

Davy Jones

Davy Jones is a British singer-songwriter, musician, actor and businessman who typically sings lead vocals for the group, though occassionally plays tambourine or maracas.

Michael Nesmith

An American songwriter, musician, actor, producer, novelist, businessman and philanthropist, you will mostly see him playing the 12-stringed guitar for the band.

Micky Dolenz

Micky Dolenz is an American actor, musician, television director, radio personality and theater director, and you can always spot him smiling from behind the drumset.

Peter Tork

Peter is also American, and when he is not on the keyboard, he plays the bass guitar.
